## Research Foundation

This folder contains the core research inputs that informed the problem framing,
analysis, and recommendations in this project. Each document represents a
distinct lens used to triangulate findings and ground design decisions.

### student_stakeholder_research.pdf
Synthesizes student perspectives on soft-skill value, development pathways,
and trust in institutional measurement tools. This research surfaced key
adoption barriers and informed constraints around credibility, engagement,
and structured support.

### soft_skill_measurement_methods.pdf
Reviews existing approaches to measuring soft skills (e.g., self-report,
peer evaluation, video analysis, AI-based methods) and analyzes their
tradeoffs in terms of scalability, bias, and feasibility for career services.

### soft_skills_workplace_impact.pdf
Establishes the importance of soft skills in workplace performance and
employability, drawing on workforce and labor-market literature. This
research provides contextual justification for why soft-skill development
and signaling matter to employers.
